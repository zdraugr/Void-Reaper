# Void Reaper

Void Reaper is a Vampire-Survivors-style "reverse bullet hell" in space — a synthwave-soaked
survival roguelite that runs in your browser with no install and no build step. You only move;
your weapons auto-fire. Survive an endless swarm, vacuum up energy to level up, draft and evolve
weapons, run quests between the chaos, and bank gold to unlock ships and permanent upgrades.

It's a single self-contained HTML file — sprites, sound effects, and the starfield are all
generated at runtime — plus one looping music track.

## Features
- **Auto-firing arsenal** — 6 base weapons, each paired with a passive that unlocks a terminal
  **evolution** (the level-up cards tell you what combos with what).
- **Ships & meta-progression** — unlock 6 distinct hulls, and spend gold on permanent perks
  (Might, Armor, Greed, Revival, …) that carry across every run.
- **Bosses** — recurring mini-bosses plus a real boss every 5 minutes that escalates: summons
  adds, fires radial bullet patterns, or both.
- **Quests & planets** — dock at a station for a cargo haul or a bounty hunt; an edge arrow
  points you to your objective.
- **Hazards** — smash asteroids for salvage; weave through indestructible obstacle belts.
- **Synthwave presentation** — neon palette, drifting nebulae, a parallax grid, CRT scanlines,
  looping music, and a past-runs log.

## How to play
- **Move:** WASD / arrow keys — or a **gamepad** (left stick / d-pad). Weapons fire automatically.
- **Dash:** Space / **A** — Afterburner, once you own the passive.
- **Pick upgrades:** 1 / 2 / 3, click, or controller (d-pad/stick + A).
- **Pause menu:** Esc / Start · **Mute:** M
- Set master & music volume (and more) in **Settings**.

Survive as long as you can, build a synergistic loadout, and chase a better time.
